SOCIAL MEDIA STRATEGY

Platform Recommendations

Facebook

Primary platform for event announcements and community engagement.

  • Post 3-4 times per week
  • Share race results within 2 hours
  • Create event pages for special races
  • Engage with fan comments daily

Instagram

Visual platform for race day photos and driver spotlights.

  • Post 2-3 times per week
  • Share action shots and winner photos
  • Use Stories for behind-the-scenes
  • Utilize racing and local hashtags

Twitter

Real-time platform for race updates and results.

  • Live tweet during races
  • Share quick results immediately
  • Engage with racing community
  • Promote special events with countdown

Content Calendar

Best Practices

  • Maintain consistent branding across all platforms
  • Respond to comments and messages within 24 hours
  • Tag sponsors and drivers in relevant posts
  • Use high-quality images that capture racing excitement
  • Create platform-specific content rather than cross-posting identical content
  • Analyze engagement metrics monthly and adjust strategy accordingly

EMAIL MARKETING STRATEGY

Campaign Types

Weekly Race Announcements

Send every Thursday to promote upcoming weekend races.

  • Featured race classes
  • Special events or promotions
  • Weather forecast
  • Ticket information

Race Results Recap

Send every Monday to summarize weekend results.

  • Top 3 finishers in each class
  • Highlight exciting moments
  • Driver quotes
  • Photo gallery link

Special Event Promotions

Send 2 weeks, 1 week, and 3 days before special events.

  • Event details and schedule
  • Special attractions
  • Early bird ticket offers
  • VIP package information

Sponsor Highlights

Send monthly to showcase sponsors.

  • Featured sponsor spotlight
  • Sponsor special offers
  • Sponsor events or news
  • Thank you message

Email Best Practices

  • Use mobile-responsive email templates
  • Keep subject lines under 50 characters
  • Include clear call-to-action buttons
  • Segment your audience based on interests (e.g., race classes)
  • Maintain consistent sending schedule
  • Include social media links in every email
  • Test different sending times to optimize open rates

EVENT PROMOTION STRATEGY

Promotion Timeline

4 Weeks Before Event

  • Initial announcement on website and social media
  • Create Facebook event page
  • Send "Save the Date" email to subscriber list
  • Begin sponsor coordination

2 Weeks Before Event

  • Detailed event email with schedule and highlights
  • Increase social media posting frequency
  • Share driver spotlights and event previews
  • Launch any early bird ticket specials

1 Week Before Event

  • Daily countdown posts on social media
  • Final promotional email with last-minute details
  • Share "behind the scenes" preparation content
  • Coordinate with local media for coverage

Event Day

  • Morning reminder posts on all platforms
  • Live updates throughout the event
  • Encourage attendee social sharing with hashtags
  • Capture content for post-event recap

Special Event Focus

Bus Races

Promotional strategy for this unique event.

  • Emphasize family-friendly entertainment
  • Share videos from previous bus races
  • Create "meet the bus drivers" content
  • Promote special ticket packages
  • Partner with local schools or businesses

Silver 1000

Promotional strategy for championship event.

  • Highlight prestigious nature of the event
  • Feature past champions and history
  • Emphasize prize money and significance
  • Create driver profiles for top contenders
  • Develop VIP experience packages

Integrated Marketing Approach

For maximum effectiveness, coordinate marketing efforts across all channels:

  • Maintain consistent messaging and branding across all platforms
  • Coordinate email, social media, and website updates to reinforce key messages
  • Create content that can be repurposed across multiple channels
  • Develop a central content calendar that all team members can access
  • Measure performance across all channels to identify most effective strategies
  • Adjust marketing mix based on seasonal needs and event importance
